# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Homeowners, residential renovation clients, and prospective collaborators in Taiwan who are evaluating X3 Design through its studio story, project imagery, services, and contact path.

## Product Purpose

The site presents X3 Design as a quiet, detail-oriented interior design studio, showcases residential project work, explains service scope, and helps qualified visitors start a design conversation.

## Brand Personality

Quiet, material, meticulous. The experience should feel calm and confident, with project photography and restrained typography carrying the sense of craft.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic agency portfolio templates, loud luxury styling, placeholder-feeling project metadata, heavy card grids, decorative effects that compete with interiors, and SaaS-like marketing patterns.

## Design Principles

Let real spaces carry trust.
Keep the interface quiet enough for material, light, and proportion to lead.
Use fewer, clearer content groups instead of fragmented sections.
Make every project page feel curated, even when some metadata is still provisional.
Preserve a calm path from browsing work to making contact.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Keep Chinese and English labels readable across mobile and desktop, preserve keyboard-accessible links and controls, maintain touch targets near 44px, and respect reduced-motion preferences for reveal effects.
